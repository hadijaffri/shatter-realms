# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

ShatterRealms is a community project where everyone should feel welcome, safe, and respected. We want this to be a place where people can learn, collaborate, and have fun building a cool game together.

Whether you're an experienced developer or just starting out, we're glad you're here!

## Our Standards

### Be Kind and Respectful

- **Be patient** with people who are learning
- **Give helpful feedback** instead of just saying something is wrong
- **Celebrate others' contributions** - everyone's work matters
- **Use welcoming language** - remember there's a real person behind every username

### Help Others Learn

- **Answer questions** when you can
- **Share your knowledge** - explain how things work
- **Suggest resources** that helped you learn
- **Encourage experimentation** - mistakes are how we learn!

### Be Professional

- **Stay on topic** in discussions
- **Accept constructive criticism** gracefully
- **Focus on what's best for the project** and community
- **Respect different opinions** and approaches

### Not Okay

These behaviors are unacceptable:

- **Bullying, harassment, or mean comments** of any kind
- **Trolling or insulting** other contributors
- **Sharing others' private information** without permission
- **Inappropriate content** (this is a game project, keep it relevant)
- **Spam or self-promotion** unrelated to the project

## Reporting Issues

If someone is violating this Code of Conduct:

1. Don't engage in arguments
2. Report it by creating a private issue or emailing the maintainers
3. Provide specific details about what happened
4. We'll review and take appropriate action

## Consequences

If someone breaks these rules:

- **First time**: Private warning and explanation
- **Second time**: Temporary ban from the project
- **Serious or repeated violations**: Permanent ban

## Questions?

Not sure if something is okay? Just ask! We're here to help create a positive community.
